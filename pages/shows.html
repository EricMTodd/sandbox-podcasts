Traditional Multiplayer Games

Game Night!

We try to get together to play tabletop simulator, or Call of Duty: Warzone, it all depends on who is available. Start time is usually sometime after 6 PM Pacific. There isn't as much of a structured time frame for this as our other productions, but every now and again they are fun to have.


5th Edition Dungeons and Dragons Campaigns

Lost Souls

Pulled into hell from random reality timelines, and forced to compete in hell's reality game show our adventurer's are trying to raise enough money to buy back their souls and return to normal life. After leaving the gladiator hell games they find themselves in Adequate Land taking every odd job possible. This game's random and bizarre world never seems to be what anyone expects it to be.

Valkenkrig: Bloodreign

It is a cursed fate, those that fall upon the shores of Valkenkrig. Evil haunts every corner while death and disease run rampant. The dead rise from their graves and the last flicker of humanity lives under the iron fist of The Falkenrath Empire. The untamed wilds are ruled by beasts, demons, and roving packs of werewolves that mercilessly prey upon those foolish enough to set foot outside the protective walls of cities and communes. All hope of freedom, all hope of prosperity is gone. The name of the game is survival; if you can't beat them, will you join them?
